Sustaining or one-time membership?

  • We think it's important that you get to support and consume the news on your own time and on your own terms. All donations are greatly appreciated.
  • A sustaining membership means your donation recurs automatically on a monthly or yearly basis via credit card. This provides the Tribune with the sort of predictable revenue that allows us to plan budgets a few years at a time, and it helps eliminate renewal mailings or lapses in your membership.

Circle Membership

  • Circle Membership is a three-year commitment that backs the vital work of our journalists. Circle members believe in supporting our public service mission so that all Texans can access our nonpartisan news, data and events at no cost.
  • Circle Members enjoy invitations to exclusive events and special updates from CEO Evan Smith.
